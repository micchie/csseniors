This script shows the author's papers published in the venues listed in CSRankings, counting those which have been published since their first first-author paper in those venues. The rational of such a list is that the first authorship indicates the work is driven and built by their own, which proves the experience to deserve some seniority. Since such a paper can be published later than others simply because of rejections, this script also considers those papers published in the previous calendar year.
